City Planner Tool

Published on 13th of September


This project was part of a course that I got from my employer, Academic Work. The final project for this course ended up being this tool, that was meant to be created for city planners in Sweden and Finland. The web application is a tool to help prevent CO2 emissions in a chosen city. The tool allows the city planners to type in a city in either Sweden or Finland, for then to have emission data from that city from past, current and future years. The future years are predicted with Machine Learning, where we the group, who made this application, trained a model to predict future emissions.
There will also be a tool showing the amount of trees needed to prevent future emissions, that will help the city planner to plan out cities and amount of trees to neautrilize emissions.
It's a pretty cool tool, and has alot of features.
